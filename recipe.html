Butter chicken

By; Sudip Regmi

The best butter chicken creamy and full of flavor

Prep Time: 10mins

CooK Time 30mins

Best servered @kumarirestaurant


Ingridients
  • 1 tablespoon oil,1 tablespoon butter,1 medium onion diced,1 teaspoon fresh ginger finely minced or grated (or use paste)
  • 2-3 cloves garlic finely minced or crushed)
  • ½ pounds about 2-3 boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into ¾-inch chunks
  • 1 cup chooped onions
  • 4 tablespoons tomato paste or 8 oz can of tomato sauce
  • 1 teaspoon Fenugreek I use powder, but seeds or mustard seeds can be used too, optional*
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • Salt and black pepper
  • Hot cooked rice and naan for serving

Recipe
  1. Heat a large skillet or medium saucepan over medium-high heat

  2. Add the oil, butter, and onions and cook onions down until lightly golden, about 3-4 .

  3. Add ginger and garlic and let cook for 30 seconds, stirring so it doesn’t burn.

  4. Spray a steamer pan with cooking spray.

  5. Add the chicken, tomato paste, and spices. Cook for 5-6 minutes or until everything is cooked through

  6. Add the heavy cream and simmer for 8-10 minutes stirring occasionally.

Serve over Basmati rice or with naan.
